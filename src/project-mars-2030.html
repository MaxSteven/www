Mars 2030

A project done over a few weeks of spare time. It is an interpretation of a line drawing by Andy Estrada (check out his work – it’s incredible!).

This is an image for all parents. Remember that when you come home from a night out and your little boy left the house in an absolute state.. He might have had a life defining moment.

Best of luck dude. Mars 2030 will be here soon.

Rendered using Arnold / AlShaders.
